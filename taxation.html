Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our taxation services

What is the difference between tax planning and tax preparation?

Tax planning is a year-round proactive process of structuring your finances to minimize tax liability. Tax preparation is the reactive process of calculating and filing taxes based on past transactions. Our services focus on forward-looking tax planning to optimize your position before transactions occur.

How often should I review my tax strategy?

We recommend reviewing your tax strategy at least quarterly, or whenever significant business changes occur (new investments, expansion, restructuring). Regular reviews ensure your strategy adapts to changing circumstances and new tax laws.

Do you work with both individuals and businesses?

Yes, we provide taxation services for individuals, sole proprietors, partnerships, corporations, and NGOs. Our team tailors solutions to the specific needs of each client type.

What is the deadline for tax filing in Nepal?

For individuals and businesses in Nepal, the tax filing deadline is typically three months after the end of the fiscal year (mid-July). We send reminders to all clients and ensure timely filing.

Do you handle international tax matters?

Yes, we have expertise in international taxation, including cross-border transactions, double taxation treaties, and tax implications for non-residents and foreign investments in Nepal.
