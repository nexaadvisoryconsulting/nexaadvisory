What is Accounting?

Accounting is the systematic process of recording, analyzing, and reporting financial transactions of a business. It provides a clear picture of your company's financial health and is essential for making informed business decisions.

At NEXA Advisory, we provide comprehensive accounting services that ensure your financial records are accurate, compliant with regulations, and ready for analysis whenever you need them.

Why is Professional Accounting Important?

  • Financial Clarity: Understand your business performance with accurate financial statements
  • Regulatory Compliance: Stay compliant with NFRS/IFRS and tax regulations
  • Business Planning: Make informed decisions based on reliable financial data
  • Stakeholder Confidence: Build trust with investors, banks, and partners

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our accounting services

What accounting software do you use?

We work with popular accounting software including Tally, QuickBooks, SAP, and various cloud-based solutions. We can also work with your existing systems or recommend the best software for your business needs.

How often will I receive financial reports?

We provide financial reports according to your needs - monthly, quarterly, or annually. Our standard package includes monthly financial statements with real-time access through our client portal.

Can you handle accounting for my small business?

Absolutely! We specialize in accounting for small and medium businesses. Our services are scalable and can be customized based on your transaction volume and specific requirements.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, we implement strict data security protocols including encrypted data transmission, secure servers, signed NDAs, and restricted access to client information. We comply with international data protection standards.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute initial consultation to understand your needs, assess your current accounting situation, and discuss how we can help your business.
