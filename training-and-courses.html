Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our training programs

Are your courses certified?

Yes, all our training programs provide certificates of completion recognized by industry bodies. Many of our courses also offer continuing professional education (CPE) credits for accounting and finance professionals.

Do you offer online training options?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online training options. Our virtual classrooms provide interactive learning experiences with live instructors, recorded sessions for review, and digital course materials.

Can you customize training for our organization?

Absolutely! We develop custom corporate training programs tailored to your organization's specific needs, industry requirements, and skill gaps. Contact us for a customized training proposal.

What is the duration of your courses?

Course durations vary from 1-day workshops to multi-week comprehensive programs ranging from 25 to 50 hours. We offer flexible scheduling including weekday, weekend, and evening batches to accommodate working professionals.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for group enrollments and corporate training packages. Discounts are available for teams of 3 or more from the same organization. Contact us for a customized quote based on your team size.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days before the course start date receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days may be subject to a 25% fee. You can also transfer your registration to a future batch at no additional cost.
