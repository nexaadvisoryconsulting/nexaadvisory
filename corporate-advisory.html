Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our corporate advisory services

How long does company registration take in Nepal?

Company registration in Nepal typically takes 5-7 working days with proper documentation. We expedite the process through our relationships with regulatory authorities and ensure all documents are prepared correctly.

What types of companies can be registered?

You can register various entities including Private Limited, Public Limited, Partnerships, Sole Proprietorships, and Non-Government Organizations (NGOs). We'll help you choose the right structure for your business.

Do you help with foreign company registration in Nepal?

Yes, we assist foreign companies with branch office registration, liaison office setup, and subsidiary formation in Nepal, ensuring compliance with FDI regulations and all legal requirements.

What is corporate restructuring and when is it needed?

Corporate restructuring involves mergers, acquisitions, demergers, and business reorganization to improve efficiency, reduce costs, or adapt to market changes. It's often needed during growth phases, ownership changes, or strategic shifts.

Do you offer ongoing compliance support?

Yes, we provide ongoing compliance support including annual ROC filings, board meeting documentation, statutory register maintenance, and advisory on regulatory changes affecting your business.
